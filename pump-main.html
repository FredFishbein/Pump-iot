The Smart Industrial Pump Concept

Predictive maintenance. Remote asset monitoring. Enhanced operational efficiency. Smart pumps and the data-driven insight they convey are delivering strategic business objectives for water and chemical treatment facilities, power plants, smart factories, and every other industrial arena.

KEY BENEFITS

Operational Efficiency • Smart pump systems include product-specific sensors that monitor your assets and store data in the cloud, enabling maintenance personnel to spend more time diagnosing and managing assets and less time in manual checkups.

Predictive maintenance • Replace preventative maintenance with analytic insights to proactively service parts before failure occurs, improving uptime and reducing life-cycle costs.

KEY BENEFITS

Minimize inefficiencies • Data is everything. With Pump IoT, you can perform root cause analysis to identify issues in your pumping operations, gain insight into pump usage to improve performance, and take data-driven action.

Remote monitoring • Measure behavior like temperature, vibration, voltage, pressure, current, and flow rate from anywhere, at any time, to improve asset performance, increase compliance adherence, and increase productivity.
